My Journey

Life to me is like an endless tunnel that one needs to explore.Like a tunnel it has hidden holes surpirising things and hideous things.It could be quite lonely and fun to explore. Its silence is intimidating and scary and at the same time quite serene. Listening to all those sounds makes you shudder or feel the adventure begins! Well that's my life just like a tunnel.At a young age my imagination took the better part of me. I stayed in a fairy tale kind of world that seemed fun and peaceful to be part of. I would have conversations with my 'band' friends,travel the world in my own private jet,sip on milkshakes beside my pool,play with my dogs Chimo and Nanook,give Tedtalks to young ladies and gents starting their carees in tech,go hiking with Lily,Deira,Tim and Zonk,Swim with Petra and Josh,Coffee with Rick,Partying with Julie,Diane,Marc,Nick,Benie and at the end of the day retire to bed in my indie bedroom with nice music from U2 and Girl in Red.
